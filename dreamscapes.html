Dreamscapes


Dreamscapes is a series of paintings that allows you to hop on my train of thought and see inside my brain.

Upon seeing an object, an animal or a person, my brain associates the thing I see with all kinds of things that pop up in my head and follow each other up at a high speed. I like to imagine my brain as a universe of memories, objects and associations, and whenever I see or hear something, the train of thought takes off and races past all kinds of memories.
This series shows my idea of the universe inside my brain and all the objects and memories that float in it.
