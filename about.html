About me






Hi! My name is Myrthe Majoor and I’m an illustrator and animator. I love animals, plants and human beings and I find joy in drawing them all. You can find me sitting on the ground and drawing almost everywhere. From the zoo to botanical gardens, and from the oldtimer garage to your local city center. If you spot a backpack in the shape of a dinosaur and a huge black sketchbook, you know it’s me!

I am currently working on ideas for a new animation film, a children’s book and some new lino prints. Follow me on Instagram to see more of my process and feel free to contact me for questions, inquiries or assignments. I’m always in for a challenge!
